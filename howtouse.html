How to use the calculator

Psyche Solar calculator is an initiative of Team Psyche of HNG Internship 6.0. The calculator determines how much energy your appliances consume and how you would set up your house if you want to generate electricity from the sun

Below are the steps:

  1. On the Add an appliance table, you input the name of your appliance e.g Television, how much of that appliance you have and how much energy the appliance(s) consume in watt, then click on the button to automatically calculate the number of solar panels you would need, inverter or UPS rating to purchase and how long your backup batteries would last
  2. Appliances can be edited on the Appliance List table, which automatically updates the previously calculated information

If you are plannig on using solar energy, this is the best app to prepare you for what's to come and if you can afford it. Rather than going straight to engineers who would advice you to buy more than you need just to make extra bucks out of you.

If TEAM PSYCHE solar calculator helped you made the right choice, refer others. Thank you from Team Psyche
